About Me

I am an Irish researcher and recent M.Sc graduate, passionate about innovation in the field of cellular and genetic medicine. As a creative thinker and motivated problem solver, I am eager to apply my skills and experience to the development of advanced therapeutics. My studies encompassing chemistry, biochemistry, molecular biology, and genetics place me in a unique position to help advance research and development projects for ATMPs.
