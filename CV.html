Experience

Montpellier Research Centre for Cell Biology
• Trainee researcher exploring the roles of co-activator complexes in gene expression regulation.

• My work involved firstly editing and transforming plasmids in yeast, to create a Cre-Lox inducible mutation system at the endogenous gene of Epl1 – a subunit of the NuA4 coactivator complex. I performed RT-qPCR on purified total RNA to analyse the effect of inducing the mutation on the expression of a regulated gene.
